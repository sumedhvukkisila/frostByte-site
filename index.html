About Our Team

FrostByte is a high school robotics team competing in the FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC). We are a team of passionate students who design, build, and program robots while learning engineering, teamwork, and problem-solving skills.

About FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC)

FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) is an international robotics program for middle and high school students.

What It’s About

  • Teams design, build, and program robots to complete game challenges announced each September.
  • Matches are played in alliances: two robots vs. two robots.
  • Robots are programmed in Java or block-based coding using sensors, motors, and controllers.

Skills Students Learn

  • Engineering & Design
  • Programming (Java, Blocks)
  • Teamwork & Leadership
  • Innovation & Problem-Solving

Competition Season

  • Kickoff: September
  • Build Season: Fall semester
  • Qualifiers: Winter tournaments
  • Championships: State, national, and world events

Core Values

Gracious Professionalism® and Coopertition® guide teams to compete fiercely while respecting and helping others.
