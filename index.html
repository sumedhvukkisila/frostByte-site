About Our Team

Mission

Inspire students to explore STEM by building competitive FTC robots and practicing gracious professionalism.

What Students Learn

  • Robot design & manufacturing (REV ecosystem)
  • Programming in Java for autonomous & tele‑op
  • CAD (Onshape/Fusion 360) and rapid prototyping
  • Project management, documentation, and outreach

Parent Snapshot

  • Practices 2–3x per week during the season
  • Competition weekends Nov–Feb
  • Estimated annual cost: parts, events, travel
  • We welcome volunteers for snacks, transport, and judging prep

Current Season

Kickoff → Strategy

Analyze the game manual, define scoring priorities, and draft a season roadmap.

Design → Prototype

CAD concepts, 3D‑print prototypes, and test mechanisms early.

Build → Iterate

Assemble chassis, wire electronics, tune code, and keep the engineering notebook current.
